Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2, 2025

Go Parks is developed and operated by Tim Sopin. This page explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

  • Anonymous analytics: The app uses Firebase Analytics to understand general usage (for example, which screens are most viewed). No personal or identifying data is collected or shared.
  • Device data: Firebase may collect basic device information such as model, OS version, and app version for diagnostic and performance purposes.

What We Don’t Collect

Go Parks does not collect or store names, emails, precise locations, or any personal identifiers. All favorites, visits, and preferences are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to servers.

Data Sharing

No personal data is sold or shared with third parties. Anonymous analytics data is processed by Google Firebase solely to help improve app quality.

Your Choices

You can disable analytics at any time from your device’s privacy settings or by uninstalling the app. When Sign in with Apple becomes available, participation will be completely optional.
